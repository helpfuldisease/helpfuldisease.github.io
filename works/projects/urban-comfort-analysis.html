Grasshopper / Ladybug

Urban Comfort Analysis

Overview

Microclimate analysis for climate-responsive open space.

A parametric environmental analysis workflow for urban open spaces, using Grasshopper and Ladybug to evaluate how spatial form, vegetation, and surface conditions affect outdoor comfort.

The study can compare design options through sunlight duration, shadow range, wind exposure, and environmental comfort indicators, translating simulation results into practical landscape design decisions.
